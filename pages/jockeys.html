For Jockeys

Jockey Resources

The Japan Racing Association provides comprehensive support and resources for jockeys at all levels of their career. From aspiring apprentices to seasoned professionals, our programs are designed to enhance skills, ensure safety, and promote career development.

We offer state-of-the-art training facilities, mentorship programs, and continuous education to help jockeys excel in their profession while maintaining the highest standards of horsemanship and sportsmanship.

Training & Development

Our jockey training programs are among the most comprehensive in the world. We provide specialized instruction in race riding techniques, pace judgment, and strategic decision-making. Training includes both classroom education and practical track experience.

Advanced programs focus on specific skills such as riding different track conditions, understanding horse behavior, and developing winning strategies for various race types and distances.

Safety & Equipment

Jockey safety is our top priority. The JRA maintains rigorous safety standards and provides the latest protective equipment. All jockeys must complete regular safety training and equipment checks to ensure optimal protection during races and training.

We continuously research and implement new safety technologies, from advanced helmet designs to impact-absorbing vests. Medical support is available at all racecourses with immediate access to specialized care when needed.

Career Opportunities

The JRA offers diverse career paths for jockeys, from domestic racing circuits to international competitions. We support jockeys in building sustainable careers with opportunities for growth and recognition at every level.

Career development includes weight management guidance, media training, and financial planning assistance. We also provide transition programs for jockeys moving into training, mentoring, or other roles within the racing industry.
